Heap

A (Min) Heap is a tree, \(T = \{R, T_{0}, T_{1}, \cdots, T_{n-1} \}\) with the following properties:
  1. Every subtree of \(T\) is a heap; and,
  2. The root of \(T\) is less than or equal to the root of every subtree of \(T\). That is, \(R \le R_{i}\) for all \(i, 0 \le i \le n\), where \(R_{i}\) is the root of \(T_{i}\).
